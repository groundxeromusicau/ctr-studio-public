# Community code of conduct

CTR Studio welcomes contributors and users of every background and experience level.

## Our standards

Positive participation includes being respectful, giving actionable feedback, accepting correction, focusing on the project’s users, and making space for people who are new to broadcasting or software development.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualised attention, deliberate disruption, publishing another person’s private information, or sustained personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, issues, and other participation that violates these standards. They may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible. Retaliation against someone who makes a good-faith report is not acceptable.

This code applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project.
